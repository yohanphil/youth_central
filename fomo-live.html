TGC Dehiwala Youth is a really fun, welcoming and Christ-centred space where people can be themselves, make real friends and grow in faith without feeling out of place, so if you’ve been thinking about checking out church or finding a youth crowd that feels alive and genuine, you should definitely come through.

Our youth cells are a really easy way to get connected because they give you a smaller, more personal space to meet people, talk, pray, grow and feel part of the family, so if a big crowd feels a bit much at first, this is one of the best ways to step in and find your people.
